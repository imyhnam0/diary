# Diary App

Express today’s feelings through drawings instead of words.
Record your hand-drawn emotion icons on the calendar
and see your emotional journey at a glance.

Even feelings that are hard to put into words
can be captured naturally with a single drawing.

Just write whatever comes to mind.
Short notes, broken sentences, and messy thoughts are all okay.

AI transforms your writing into a smooth, heartfelt daily journal.

Even on days when writing feels difficult,
keep your journaling habit going with ease.
